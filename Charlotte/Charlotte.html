A downloadable game for Windows

A 2d game made in Unity about becoming a ghost.

After dieing, meet a butterfly willing to guide you away from the forest. But all you really want is to be able to see your fammily one last time. Embark on a journey to find them again. Fight monsters and solve puzzles.

Sometimes the game can trigger antivirus software ect because it is unknown so keep that in mind and enjoy!
