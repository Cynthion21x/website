Short Description:

BeeTD is a tower defence game all about protecting yourself from summer's greatest menace. The wasp. Do not let the cute visuals fool you! The wasps have no mercy. Collect items to make your insects reach incredible strength!

Long Description:

BeeTD is a difficult tower defence game about fighting wasps! There is a bunch of powers to pick from, annoying wind pushing away all your insects and much more.

Each power comes with a unique set of insects that come with it giving you lots of diffrent playstyles to try out. Each power also comes with its own challange to complete to unlock new insects to use!

After you beat or boss or find an encounter with an item wasp you have an oppertunity to pick 1 of free items. Some more usefull than others...
